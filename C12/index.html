    listening-1

    I'd like to find out if you have any excursions suitable for families.

    excursion [ɪk'skɜːʃ(ə)n] 远足

    suitable 这种后置修饰要注意别漏听

    How about taking your family for a cruise? We have a steamship that takes passengers out several times a day.

    cruise [kruːz] 巡游

    这个听起来会很绕，注意这种时间的讲法。

    奇艺的，新奇的

    various

    多种多样的，a variety of

    meet sb off the boat 接下船

    holding pens 圈养

    keep 饲养

    trek 艰苦跋涉

    round 跟all year round不同，这里应该是介词：绕

    on their own 任他们自己

    as long as 只要

    take care to do sth

    There are some very beautiful gardens along the side of the lake which also belong to the farm.

    along the side of 沿着xx的一面（边）

    trail [treɪl] （注意读音）小径

    in terms of 在...方面

    work out

    解决

    It's just by the cruise ship terminal.

    by 附近，旁边

    repair kit 修理工具包

    snack [snæk] 小吃，快餐。 Shaxian snack哈哈

    helmet ['helmɪt] 头盔

    you should definitely get that.

    definitely ['defɪnɪtlɪ] 当然地。口语时省略读te, 就像definely

    what sort of prices are we looking at here?

    look at 这里是考虑之意
